Get Pre-Qualified

Getting pre-qualified is your first step toward homeownership. It gives you a clear understanding of how much you can afford and strengthens your position when making an offer.

Benefits of Pre-Qualification:

  • Know your exact budget before house hunting
  • Show sellers you're a serious buyer
  • Speed up the closing process significantly
  • Identify and resolve credit issues early

Our pre-qualification process is quick and easy, typically completed within 24-48 hours. We'll review your income, assets, and credit to determine your buying power.

Loan Programs Available

We offer a comprehensive range of mortgage programs designed to meet diverse financial situations and homeownership goals.

Our Loan Options:

  • Conventional Loans: Traditional financing with competitive rates for qualified buyers
  • FHA Loans: Low down payment options (as low as 3.5%) for first-time buyers
  • VA Loans: Zero down payment loans for eligible veterans and service members
  • USDA Loans: 100% financing for rural property purchases
  • Jumbo Loans: Financing for high-value properties exceeding conventional limits
  • ARM Loans: Adjustable-rate mortgages with lower initial rates

Each program has unique benefits and requirements. Our mortgage specialists will help you choose the best option for your situation.

Refinancing Options

Refinancing can help you secure a lower interest rate, reduce monthly payments, or access your home's equity for important expenses.

Why Refinance?

  • Lower your interest rate and save thousands over the loan term
  • Switch from an adjustable-rate to a fixed-rate mortgage
  • Shorten your loan term to build equity faster
  • Cash-out refinance to fund home improvements or debt consolidation
  • Remove PMI (Private Mortgage Insurance) when you reach 20% equity

Current market conditions may present excellent refinancing opportunities. Let us analyze your situation and show you potential savings.

First-Time Home Buyers

Buying your first home is an exciting milestone. We specialize in guiding first-time buyers through every step of the process with patience and expertise.

Special Programs for First-Time Buyers:

  • Down payment assistance programs (DPA)
  • First-time buyer grants and tax credits
  • Lower down payment options (as low as 3%)
  • Flexible credit requirements
  • Educational resources and homebuyer workshops

We'll Help You:

  • Understand the entire mortgage process from start to finish
  • Calculate affordability and create a realistic budget
  • Navigate closing costs and prepare for unexpected expenses
  • Build credit if needed to qualify for better rates

Third Party Financing

Beyond traditional mortgage options, we connect you with alternative financing solutions through our network of trusted third-party lenders and investors.

Alternative Financing Options:

  • Private Money Lenders: Fast approval for unique situations or properties
  • Hard Money Loans: Asset-based lending for investment properties or fix-and-flip projects
  • Portfolio Loans: Custom financing held by the lender, not sold to investors
  • Seller Financing: Direct agreements with property sellers
  • Bridge Loans: Short-term financing to bridge gaps between transactions
  • Non-QM Loans: Alternative documentation for self-employed or non-traditional income

Ideal For:

  • Real estate investors needing quick financing
  • Borrowers with credit challenges or unique situations
  • Commercial property purchases
  • Properties that don't qualify for conventional financing

The Mortgage Process

Understanding the mortgage process helps reduce stress and ensures a smooth path to homeownership. Here's what to expect when working with Quinnes Mortgage.

Step-by-Step Process:

  1. Pre-Qualification (1-2 days)

    Submit basic financial information for an initial assessment of your buying power.

  2. Pre-Approval (3-5 days)

    Complete application with documentation. Receive conditional approval letter for house hunting.

  3. House Hunting

    Find your dream home within your approved budget with confidence.

  4. Purchase Agreement

    Make an offer and negotiate terms with the seller.

  5. Loan Processing (10-14 days)

    Submit final documentation. Lender orders appraisal and title search.

  6. Underwriting (5-7 days)

    Final review and approval of your loan application and supporting documents.

  7. Clear to Close

    Receive final approval and schedule your closing date.

  8. Closing Day

    Sign documents, receive keys, and celebrate your new home!

Average Timeline: 30-45 days from application to closing, though this can vary based on your situation.
