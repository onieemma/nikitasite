Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need insurance for a rental property?

Yes, insurance protects both the property and your tenants, covering damages and liability risks.

What disasters are typically covered?

Common coverage includes fire, storms, flood, theft, and accidental damages, depending on the policy.

How quickly can I get insured?

While this varies by provider, understanding your property and coverage needs ahead of time speeds up the process.

Can foreigners insure property?

Many insurance providers allow non-residents to obtain property coverage, though requirements differ by location.
